Site Name

Site Name: DevJourneyPortfolio.com

Reason for this Site Name: The site name clearly reflects the purpose of the website, which is to showcase my web development portfolio. It's short, memorable, and aligns with my goal of creating a professional online presence as a web developer.

Site Purpose

Purpose of the Site: The purpose of this website is to act as a professional portfolio to display the web development projects I've worked on. It serves as a platform to showcase my learning progress and provides a means for potential employers or clients to view my skills, projects, and contact information.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: How can I contact you for freelance web development projects?

Scenario 2: What are the technologies you've worked with, and can I see examples of your projects?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #47E5BC (Used for header and important buttons/sections)

Secondary Color: #023436 (Used for navigation bar, footer, and hover effects)

Accent Colors: #60435F (Used for navigation bar), #FF0022 (Accent color for buttons or important elements)

Background Color: #FFEEDB (Used for the overall page background)

The primary green (#47E5BC) creates a fresh and modern feel, while the dark teal (#023436) is used for navigation and footer to create contrast. The purple (#60435F) adds a touch of elegance to the navigation bar, and the bright red (#FF0022) can be used sparingly to highlight important buttons or calls to action.

Typography

Primary Font: Poppins, used for body text and paragraphs. Poppins is a clean, sans-serif font that enhances readability and is highly versatile.

Secondary Font: Work Sans, used for headings and titles. Work Sans has a modern, professional look and is great for making headings stand out.
